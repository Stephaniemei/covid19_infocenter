What is Pfizer-BioNTech vaccine ?


General Information

Name: BNT16262

Manufacturer: Pfizer, Inc., and BioNTech

Type of vaccine: mRNA

Number of shots: 2 shots, 21 days apart

How given: Shot in the muscle of the under arm

Does not Contain: Eggs, Preservatives and latex

How Pfizer-BioNTech works ?

The vaccine works by introducing a molecule called messenger RNA(mRNA) into the sell. This molecule teaches cells to make proteins from the virus that causes COVID-19, known as SARS-CoV-2.

The human body detects this protein and triggers an immune response. This will produce antibodies and longer-term immunity, which can resist future SARS-CoV-2 infections.

How effective is the Pfizer-BioNTech vaccine ?

It is important to distinguish between its efficacy and its effectiveness when considering the impact of the Pfizer vaccine.

Efficacy

There is strong evidence from clinical trials that the Pfizer vaccine has a high level of efficacy.

A trial involving 43,548 participants funded by BioNTech and Pfizer reported that their 2 doses of vaccine provide people over 16 years of age with 95% protection against COVID-19.

Effectiveness

The results are consistent across all age groups, gender, race, race, and weight, as well as people with basic medical conditions and people who have been infected with SARS-CoV-2.

A large-scale study of Pfizer vaccines was conducted in Israel, involving approximately 1.2 million people, and this evidence supports the results of clinical trials. This shows that 7 days after receiving the second dose of vaccine, two doses of vaccine can reduce symptomatic COVID-19 cases by 94% in all age groups. The vaccine also reduced 92% of serious diseases.

Benefits of Pfizer-BioNTech vaccine



The main benefit of getting the COVID-19 vaccine is to prevent the disease, its symptoms and complications.

COVID-19 has a wide range of effects on the body. For some people, this is a mild short-term illness. For others, it is serious and can be life-threatening. Some people will also experience prolonged COVID, which may last for weeks or months.

The vaccinated person is also unlikely to spread the virus that causes COVID-19 to other people, thereby reducing the risk of other people, especially vulnerable people.

What are the ingredients in the Pfizer-BioNTech COVID-19 Vaccine ?

  • mRNA
  • lipids ((4-hydroxybutyl)azanediyl)bis(hexane-6,1-diyl)bis(2-hexyldecanoate), 2[(polyethylene glycol)-2000]-N,N-ditetradecylacetamide, 1,2-Distearoyl-sn-glycero-3-phosphocholine, and cholesterol)
  • monobasic potassium phosphate and dibasic sodium phosphate dehydrate, which act as buffers
  • potassium chloride and sodium chloride, which are types of salt
  • Fever
  • sucrose, or table sugar

Who should get the Pfizer-BioNTech vaccine ?

Recommended for individuals 16 years of age and older.

Who should NOT get the Pfizer-BioNTech vaccine ?

Indivuduals that had:

  • severe allergic reaction after a previous dose of this vaccine
  • severe allergic reaction to any ingredient of this vaccine

What should be mentioned to vaccination provider before you get the Pfizer-BioNTech vaccine ?



Tell them all of your medical conditions, including if you:

  • have fever
  • have any allergies
  • have bleeding disorder
  • have received another Covid-19 Vaccine
  • are immunocompromised
  • are pregnant
  • are breastfeeding

Possible Side effects

The side effects usually will start within a day or two after vaccination, but they will disappear after a few days.

On the arm where you got the shot:

  • Pain
  • Redness
  • Swelling

On the rest of the body:

  • Tiredness
  • Headache
  • Muscle pain
  • Chills
  • Fever
  • Nausea
